Mama's Great Chili

Description

Who can say no to a big bowl of warm chili on a cold winter day? I know that I can't and I'll bet you're the same way. This simple chili recipe will warm your insides and raise your spirits during the long cold winter.

There's a secret to this chili that makes it even better! It's got delicious butternut squash. The squash adds an amazing flavor and makes the bowl of chili extra nutrient rich. Even if your family says they don't like butternut squash, they will love this chili.

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Paste
  • Hot Sauce
  • Broth
  • Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Cornmeal
  • Butternut Squash

Instructions

  1. Combine the spices, then add half of them to hot oil in a Dutch Oven. Cook for 30 seconds to toast the spices, then add the onion and pepper and cook until soft.
  2. Add in the ground beef and the remainder of the spices. Cook until the beef is cooked through.
  3. Add in the tomato paste and cook it until it is fragrant. Add in the hot sauce, 2 cups of broth, the beans, the tomatoes, and the cornmeal. Add in the butternut squash, then if needed, more of the broth.
  4. Cover the pot and cook for 45 minutes. Remove the lid and let the chili cook for another 15 minutes. Check the squash and continue to cook if it is not yet soft. Taste and season with salt and pepper.
