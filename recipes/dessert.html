The Sweet Adults Hate

Adults seem to have no problem pulling items out of the cupboards and putting them together to make a delicious meal. The only thing is, they don't usually choose to make something sweet. Rather than waiting on the adults, a kids can go through the cupboards and pick out the sweet items to combine to make a dessert of their own choosing.

Ingrediants

  • Granulated Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Quik
  • Chocolate Sprinkles
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. Fill a cup with milk
  2. Add one spoonful of granulated sugar
  3. Add one spoonful of brown sugar
  4. Add two spoonfuls of chocolate quik
  5. Fill the top of the cup with whipped cream
  6. Add chocolate sprinkles
  7. Offer to share your sweet concoction
