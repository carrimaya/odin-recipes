Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Sugar
  • Sea Salt
  • Butter
  • Buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 425F.
  2. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt in the bowl of a food processor. Pulse three to four times so that it is mixed.
  3. Cut the cold butter into cubes or think slices, then scatter it over the flour in the food processor. Pulse 5 to 7 times or until the butter turns into tiny bits.
  4. Empty the butter-flour mixture into a large bowl. Make a well in the middle, and then pour in the milk. Stir until a shaggy dough forms.
  5. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured work surface. Sprinkle a little flour on top, and then bring the dough together with your hands. It might be a bit sticky, so add flour as needed.
  6. Cut out the biscuits.
  7. Bake the biscuits until golden brown and have risen, 10 to 15 minutes. Serve warm.
